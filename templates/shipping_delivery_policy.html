Delivery Instructions

For Customers:

  • Please provide accurate delivery address and contact number
  • Keep someone available at the delivery address during the scheduled time
  • Check the package for any damage before accepting
  • Store meals in refrigerator immediately upon delivery
  • Consume within the recommended time frame

Special Instructions:

  • For office deliveries, please specify building and floor details
  • For gated communities, provide security guard instructions
  • For dietary restrictions, mention during order placement

Delivery Issues

What to do if:

  • Delivery is delayed: Contact us immediately. We'll provide updates and compensation if needed.
  • Package is damaged: Do not accept damaged packages. We'll arrange a replacement.
  • Food temperature issues: If food isn't at proper temperature, contact us for replacement.
  • No one available: We'll attempt delivery twice. After that, a re-delivery fee applies.

Returns & Refunds

  • Quality Issues: Full refund or replacement for quality problems
  • Wrong Items: Immediate replacement with correct items
  • Allergies/Intolerances: Full refund if dietary requirements not met
  • Delivery Problems: Refund of delivery charges for service failures
  • Time Frame: Report issues within 2 hours of delivery
