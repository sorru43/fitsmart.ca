Icon Design Guidelines

Regular Icons (any purpose):

  • • Use your brand logo or symbol
  • • Keep it simple and recognizable
  • • Ensure good contrast with background
  • • Test at small sizes (16x16, 32x32)

Maskable Icons:

  • • Add 10% padding around your icon
  • • Design for circular/square masks
  • • Keep important elements in center
  • • Test with different mask shapes
