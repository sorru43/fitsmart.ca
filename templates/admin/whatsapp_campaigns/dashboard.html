Steps to configure:

  1. Go to Meta Developer Console
  2. Navigate to your WhatsApp Business App
  3. Go to Webhooks section
  4. Add the webhook URL above
  5. Set verify token to match your configuration
  6. Subscribe to message and status_update events
WhatsApp Template Guidelines
Template Requirements:
  • Templates must be pre-approved by WhatsApp
  • Cannot contain promotional content initially
  • Must follow WhatsApp's content policies
  • Variables are marked with {{1}}, {{2}}, etc.
Best Practices:
  • Keep messages concise and clear
  • Use emojis sparingly and appropriately
  • Include clear call-to-action when needed
  • Test templates before sending to users
Template Types:
  • Text Templates: Simple text messages
  • Media Templates: Include images, documents, or videos
  • Interactive Templates: Include buttons or quick replies
